FAQ

Where should I start?

Start in the playground, then pick the framework tab that matches your stack.

How do I keep effects lightweight?

Use shorter trails, lower particle size, and moderate fade/chase settings for smoother rendering.

Can I use the same visual style across frameworks?

Yes. Configure once in Wispr and export equivalent snippets for JavaScript, React, Vue, and Angular.
