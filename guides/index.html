Pick your stack and wire in the snippet from the matching playground tab. Everything the designer can do today is summarized below.

Playground features

  • Canvas — live preview with dark, light, or gradient background.
  • Presets — Galaxy, Fire, Candy, Neon, Snow, Gold, or start from scratch.
  • Look — particle shapes, up to three colors, gradient / mixed / random modes, blend modes, optional glow.
  • Motion — trail length, size, fade, compactness, scatter, rotation, gravity, vertical offset.
  • Chase & lag — optional lagging trail: smooth, elastic, or delayed follow.
  • Export — copy or download JS, CSS, HTML, React, Vue, and Angular snippets.
